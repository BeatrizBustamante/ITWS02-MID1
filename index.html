About Belle

Belle, the book-loving daughter of an eccentric inventor, yearns to abandon her predictable village life in return for adventure. -Beauty and the Beast (1991).

Belle's Characteristics

  1. She is spirited, eccentric, and kind.
  2. She is confident and comfortable being herself.
  3. She knows it takes courage and kindness to see beauty where others don't.
